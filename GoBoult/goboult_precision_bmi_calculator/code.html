What is BMI?

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a clinical measurement of person's weight with respect to their height. It is more of an indicator than a direct measurement of a person's total body fat.

analytics
Metric Reliability

While accurate for most adults, BMI may overestimate body fat in athletes and others who have a muscular build, or underestimate it in older persons who have lost muscle mass.
